NetBoot.xyz

During my weekly extracurricular research, I stubbled upon NetBoot.xyz and an accompanying video. With NetBoot.xyz you can try MANY OS in your computer without having to install it (in most cases). Why is this something I am recommending? We'll, no need for VM's!

Also as the video suggested, you can do away without USB's to have a fresh install of any OS you choose.

Just reboot in NIC Boot Mode with PXE enabled and done!
You have lots of OS to test drive. EZ! Just be careful not to wipe your hard drive accidentally. That is the only caution I'd give. Other than that, you can try as many OS NetBoot.xyz has to offer in minutes.
